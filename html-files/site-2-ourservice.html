About Us

My wife and I decided to start this company when it became clear this was our new permanent home. We have lived in this beautiful forest for almost twenty years now, and have been perfecting our coffee blend for most of that time. About two years after our arrival, we found a beautiful plant with dark seeds scattered around the base of it. The indigenous animals seemed to enjoy this delectable treat, leaving only remnants of a few seeds behind. This was the only thing we had found up until that point that wasn’t blue. Sarah has always loved coffee, and suggested we attempt to create our own. She has always been fascinated with the ways of the forest, and I love helping her start a new project. At first, that’s all it was: a project. However, both of us enjoyed it so much that we started growing our own plants, in a controlled area protected from the reach of the curious creatures. With ever increasing numbers of Humans starting to settle here, we have started to mass produce this decadent beverage, as well as diversify our blends. Even some of our alien friends enjoy a few of the versions. While nothing we have yet discovered on Blue even comes close to matching the bitterness of Earth coffee, our locally grown blends are the perfect start to any day.

Fresh Coffees

Freshly ground coffee tends to have a better onsistency and overall quality compared to pre-ground beans. This has to do, in part, with the moisture that's naturally present from oils in coffee beans. These oils, when dissolved in water, contribute to a great-smelling and great-tasting cup of coffee.
